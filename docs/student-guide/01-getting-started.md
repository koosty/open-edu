## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Access the Login Page
1. Navigate to the Open-EDU platform URL provided by your institution
2. Click on the **"Sign In"** button in the top-right corner
3. You'll be directed to the authentication page

### Step 2: Sign In with Your Credentials
Open-EDU uses Firebase Authentication, which supports multiple sign-in methods:

- **Email/Password**: Enter your email and password
- **Google Account**: Click "Sign in with Google" for quick access
- **Other Providers**: Your institution may have enabled additional authentication methods

> **First-time users**: If you don't have an account yet, click "Create Account" or follow the registration link provided by your instructor.

### Step 3: Complete Your Profile
After your first login:
1. Go to your **Profile** (click your avatar in the top-right corner)
2. Add or update your display name
3. Upload a profile picture (optional)
4. Review your account settings

## Exploring the Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll land on your **Dashboard** - your central hub for all learning activities.

### Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard displays:

1. **My Courses Section**
   - All courses you're currently enrolled in
   - Course progress for each (percentage completed)
   - Quick "Continue Learning" buttons

2. **Quick Stats**
   - Total courses enrolled
   - Overall progress
   - Recent activity

3. **Recent Activity**
   - Your latest lessons viewed
   - Recent quiz attempts
   - Completed lessons

4. **Announcements** (if enabled)
   - Important updates from instructors
   - Platform notifications

### Navigation Menu

The main navigation includes:
- **Dashboard**: Your home page
- **Courses**: Browse all available courses
- **Profile**: Manage your account settings
- **Help**: Access documentation and support

## Browsing Available Courses

### Step 1: Access the Course Catalog
1. Click on **"Courses"** in the main navigation
2. You'll see a list of all available courses

### Course Catalog Features

**Course Cards Display:**
- Course title and description
- Instructor name
- Number of lessons
- Duration estimate
- Enrollment status (Enrolled or Available)

**Filtering & Search** (if available):
- Search by course name or keyword
- Filter by category or difficulty level
- Sort by newest, most popular, or recommended

### Viewing Course Details
Click on any course card to view:
- Complete course description
- Full syllabus with lesson list
- Prerequisites (if any)
- Learning objectives
- Instructor information
- Student reviews (if enabled)

## Enrolling in a Course

### Self-Enrollment Process

1. **Navigate to the course** you want to take
2. Click the **"Enroll in Course"** button
3. Confirm your enrollment
4. You'll be redirected to the course page

> **Note**: Some courses may require instructor approval or have prerequisites. In such cases, you'll see enrollment requirements before you can join.

### After Enrollment

Once enrolled, you can:
- Start learning immediately
- Access all course materials
- Track your progress
- Take quizzes and assessments
- Save notes and bookmarks

## Understanding Course Structure

### Course Hierarchy

```
Course
├── Lesson 1: Introduction
│   ├── Reading content
│   ├── Optional quiz
│   └── Notes section
├── Lesson 2: Core Concepts
│   ├── Reading content
│   ├── Code examples
│   └── Quiz assessment
└── Lesson 3: Advanced Topics
    ├── Reading content
    ├── Interactive examples
    └── Final assessment
```

### Lesson Types

Open-EDU supports different lesson types:

1. **Text Lessons**
   - Reading material with markdown formatting
   - Support for headings, lists, links, images
   - Code syntax highlighting

2. **Quiz Lessons**
   - Assessment with multiple question types
   - Timed or untimed
   - Multiple attempts allowed (varies by instructor)

3. **Mixed Lessons**
   - Combination of reading content and quiz
   - Study first, then test your knowledge

### Progress Tracking

Your progress is automatically tracked:
- **Reading Progress**: Percentage of lesson scrolled
- **Completion Status**: Marked complete when you reach the end
- **Quiz Scores**: Recorded for each attempt
- **Course Progress**: Overall percentage across all lessons

## Navigation Basics

### Getting to Your Courses

**From Dashboard:**
- Click on any course card
- Click "Continue Learning" to resume where you left off

**From Course Page:**
- View the full lesson list
- Click any lesson to start learning
- See your progress for each lesson

### Inside a Lesson

The lesson page includes:

1. **Top Navigation Bar**
   - Course title (click to return to course overview)
   - Lesson title
   - Auto-save indicator (your progress is saved automatically)

2. **Sidebar (Left)**
   - Full lesson list
   - Completion indicators (✓ for completed)
   - Search lessons (press `/` shortcut)
   - Current lesson highlighted

3. **Main Content Area**
   - Lesson content
   - Scroll to read
   - Reading progress indicator

4. **Bottom Navigation**
   - "Previous Lesson" button
   - "Next Lesson" button
   - "Mark as Complete" button (if not auto-marked)

5. **Additional Features**
   - Notes panel (bottom sheet)
   - Bookmark button
   - Reading mode toggle
   - Focus mode (hide sidebar with `Cmd+S` or `Ctrl+S`)

### Keyboard Shortcuts

Speed up your navigation with keyboard shortcuts:

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `/` | Search lessons in sidebar |
| `←` | Previous lesson |
| `→` | Next lesson |
| `Cmd+S` / `Ctrl+S` | Toggle focus mode |
| `Esc` | Close modals/panels |
